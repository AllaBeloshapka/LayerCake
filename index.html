LayerCake

A responsive business website for a custom cake shop.

LayerCake combines a customer-facing cake shop experience with admin tools for orders, reviews, product management, and business statistics.

Business value

What this project does for a small business

Customer-friendly cake browsing

Customers can explore cakes, prices, descriptions, and product details from mobile, tablet, or desktop screens.

Digital order flow

The project includes an order flow that helps collect customer requests and keeps orders visible for the business owner.

Reviews and trust

Approved customer reviews help future customers understand the quality of the service before placing an order.
